    Bookkeeping

    The financial records of your business are the backbone of your operations. I provide comprehensive bookkeeping services to ensure your finances are accurately tracked and maintained. From managing income and expenses to preparing financial statements, I handle all the details so you can focus on what you do best.

    Payroll

    Payroll processing is a critical, tedious, function for any employer. Writing checks or direct deposit every week or two; and the constant remittance of employment taxes. It takes time and needs to be done correctly. It's your livlihood and the livlihood of the people you support. I can help you manage your payroll efficiently and ensure compliance with all federal and state regulations. There are many moving parts behind the paychecks that go out every 2 weeks. Whether you've got tipped employees or retirement plan contributions, I can help you navigate the complexities of each payday.
