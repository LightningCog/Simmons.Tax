Tax Services

Personal & Business tax compliance

As an Enrolled Agent, I have the highest credential the IRS awards. My status grants me unlimited practice rights to represent taxpayers before the IRS. The same ability as an attorney or CPA, except my credential derives authority from the federal government, instead of a state issued license, allowing more flexibility across state lines.

Personal

Need help with your personal taxes? I offer comprehensive tax preparation services tailored to your unique situation. From W-2s to 1099s, I ensure you get the maximum refund possible while staying compliant with all tax laws. The IRS and state departments of revenue filing requirements can be complex, but I'm here to guide you through the process.

Business

Running a business comes with its own set of tax challenges. I provide expert tax planning and preparation services for small to medium-sized businesses. From entity selection to deductions and credits, I help you navigate the complexities of business taxes, ensuring compliance and maximizing your tax benefits.

Other tax entities, such as non-profits, estates, and home owners associations, have unique filing requirements and tax implications. I can assist annual fillings of all these nonstandard entities to ensure ongoing tax compliance.
